Muscle Building

"Muscle building," also known as resistance training or strength training, refers to the process of increasing muscle size and strength through exercise and diet. This type of training typically involves using resistance, such as weights, resistance bands, or body weight, to stimulate muscles to grow and adapt.

Body Building

Bodybuilding is a specialized form of physical fitness training focused on developing and sculpting muscles through intense resistance training, specific nutrition strategies, and often supplementation. Unlike general fitness or strength training, bodybuilding emphasizes achieving a highly muscular and defined physique.
